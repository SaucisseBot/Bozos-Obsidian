# Overview

The farmer's job is focused on semi-active resource production.
Their goal will be to obtain different types of vegetables and crops to craft complex and varied dishes.

The job will be quite repetitive but chill, and will primarily focus on planning and prevision.

# Gameplay

* Maintaining a garden => Passive
* Outdoor crops rotation => Solo
* Farming mission using specialised tool => Group / Solo
