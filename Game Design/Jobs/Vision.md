# Jobs - Vision

Professions are one of the core pillars of the game.

They are not side activities designed to support combat progression.

Instead, each profession represents a complete way to experience the world, offering its own gameplay loops, progression systems, rewards, challenges, and identity.

A player should be able to build their entire journey around a profession they genuinely enjoy.

Whether they choose to become a fisherman, a hunter, a craftsman, a farmer, or any future profession, their choice should feel meaningful and respected by the game.

## Meaningful Progression

Progression should reward dedication without creating excessive power gaps.

Advancing a profession should unlock:

* New activities
* New regions
* New mechanics
* New tools
* New quests
* New stories

The goal is to expand possibilities rather than simply increase numbers.

---

## Social Interdependence

Professions should naturally encourage interaction between players.

Players should benefit from cooperation without becoming dependent on mandatory group play.

The ideal profession ecosystem creates opportunities for:

* Trading
* Collaboration
* Specialization
* Mentorship
* Community events

Every profession should contribute something valuable to the wider world.


## Design Goal

Professions should not exist to support another system.

Professions should be worth playing for their own sake.

A player should be able to spend an entire evening engaging with their favorite profession and feel that their time was meaningful, rewarding, and fun.
