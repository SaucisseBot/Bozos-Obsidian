# Dungeons - Vision


Dungeons are the primary cooperative activity of the combat profession.

They are designed to combine the handcrafted feeling of traditional MMORPG dungeons with the replayability of modern roguelikes.

A player's first experience of a dungeon is a curated adventure tied to a specific region, story, and atmosphere. Subsequent runs become part of a modular system that generates new challenges through fragment combinations, boss choices, affixes, and difficulty scaling.

The objective is simple:

> Every run should feel familiar enough to be mastered, but different enough to remain interesting.
