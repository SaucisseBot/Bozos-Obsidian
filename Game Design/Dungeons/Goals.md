# Dungeons - Design Goals

* Infinite replayability through modular content
* Meaningful player choice
* Strong cooperative gameplay
* Low content obsolescence
* Casual accessibility
* Competitive depth
* Consistency over optimization
* Fun mechanics over numerical scaling

The ideal dungeon system should allow players to complete hundreds of runs while continuously encountering new combinations, new decisions, and new stories.
