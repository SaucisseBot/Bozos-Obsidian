Each dungeon is initially experienced as a handcrafted adventure.

During this first completion, players discover:

* The zone's story
* Unique enemies
* Boss mechanics
* Environmental design
* Dungeon-specific encounters

Players may complete these dungeons with friends, matchmaking, or NPC companions.

Completing a dungeon permanently unlocks its fragments for the Modular Dungeon System.
