# Dungeons - Seasonal Challenges and Rewards


## Seasonal Challenges

For players seeking competition, each season introduces a set of fixed dungeon seeds.



All players receive the exact same dungeon configurations.

No random generation is involved.

Success depends entirely on:

* Skill
* Strategy
* Knowledge
* Teamwork

This creates a fair environment for leaderboards and speedrunning.

---

## Seasonal Rewards

Two reward tracks exist.

### Competitive Rewards

Granted to top-performing players.

Examples:

* Titles
* Mounts
* Cosmetics
* Profile Badges

### Participation Rewards

Granted to all players who reach a seasonal milestone.

Examples:

* Cosmetics
* Housing Decorations
* Collectibles

The system rewards excellence without excluding casual players.
