## Target Audience

Our target audience is primarily young adults and teenagers, roughly between 12 and 28 years old, who enjoy video games in a casual and social way.

They are looking for an experience that can naturally fit into their daily lives without requiring constant commitment or long mandatory play sessions.

They want a game they can enjoy at their own pace, whether they play a few hours every day or only a few times each week.

Unlike highly competitive MMORPGs, they are not seeking a second job. They want a world they can return to whenever they have free time and still feel that their presence matters.

Many of these players enjoy sharing their gaming experiences with others. They often play alongside friends, siblings, partners, or family members, and appreciate games that allow people with different levels of experience and available time to play together comfortably.

Customization and self-expression are also important to them. They enjoy creating unique characters, collecting cosmetic items, decorating personal spaces, and developing an identity that reflects their personality within the game world.

This audience generally values:

* Social interaction and cooperation
* Playing with friends and family
* Character customization and personalization
* Exploration and discovery
* Relaxed progression
* Meaningful long-term goals
* Flexible play sessions
* Fair monetization

They are not primarily motivated by competition, rankings, or optimization. Instead, they seek memorable experiences, shared adventures, and a world that feels welcoming and alive.

The ideal player logs in because they want to spend time in the world and with its community, not because they feel forced to keep up with an endless progression race.
