# Core Principles

Every design decision should respect these principles.

---

## Social Over Efficiency

Player interaction should be encouraged whenever possible.

When a mechanic creates meaningful social experiences, that is the stronger choice.

---

## Journey Matters More Than Destination

The path should be enjoyable.

Players should not feel pushed to rush toward an endgame before the game becomes fun.

---

## Cooperation as the Foundation

Competition can exist.

Cooperation should remain the basis of the world.

---

## Freedom Over Optimization

Players should be able to choose their own path.

No activity should be mandatory for progression.

---

## Fun Over Rewards

Activities should be enjoyable because of the experience itself.

Rewards should reinforce fun, not replace it.

---

## Humans Over Systems

Game systems exist to create meaningful interactions between players.

They should never take priority over the people using them.

---

## Horizontal Growth Over Vertical Growth

Players should gain options, experiences, and specialization.

They should not gain overwhelming power that separates them from others.

---

## Community Is The Endgame

The ultimate goal of the project is not gear, levels, or wealth.

It is the creation of a living community where players enjoy spending time together.
