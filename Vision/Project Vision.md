# Project Vision


## What We Want to Build
  
This project aims to recreate the feeling of the MMORPGs that defined a generation.  
  
Games such as [[Nostale]], [[Dofus]], [[Dragonica]], [[Elsword]], and early [[World of Warcraft]] created experiences that players still remember years later.  
  
Their strength was not only their content.  
  
It was the sense of discovery, the social atmosphere, and the stories created between players.  
  
Our goal is not to build a competitive MMO.  
  
Our goal is not to create an endless gear treadmill.  
  
Our goal is to create a living online world where players log in because they genuinely enjoy spending time there.  
  
A place where exploration, friendship, cooperation, and shared adventures matter more than optimization.  
  
---  
  
## Our Philosophy
  
The game should encourage players to enjoy the journey rather than rush toward an endgame.  
  
Progression should be:  
  
- Slow  
- Meaningful  
- Horizontal  
- Non-frustrating  
  
Every activity should have value, even when it does not directly increase power.  
  
Helping another player, participating in an event, fishing with friends, or exploring a new area should all feel like worthwhile experiences.  
  
---  
  
## Social First
  
The project is built around a simple idea:  
  
> A player should always have a good reason to play with another player.  
  
Regardless of:  
  
- Level  
- Playtime  
- Experience  
- Preferred activities  
- Skill level  
  
The game should constantly encourage interaction, cooperation, and community building.  
  
Meeting a stranger should be just as valuable as playing with a long-time friend.  
  
---  
  
## Long-Term Goal
  
The ultimate objective is to create an MMORPG where players remember the people they met and the stories they lived more than the rewards they obtained.

A world where community becomes the true endgame.
