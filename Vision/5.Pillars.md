# Design Pillars

## Social First

The game exists to create meaningful interactions between players.

Every system should encourage cooperation, shared experiences, and community building.

People are the most important content in the game.

---

## The Journey Is The Reward

Progression is not a race toward an endgame.

Players should enjoy the experience of playing, exploring, and discovering at every stage of their adventure.

The path matters more than the destination.

---

## Build Your Own Story

Players are free to create their own journey through professions, exploration, social activities, collections, achievements, and personal goals.

There is no single "correct" way to play.

---

## Every Profession Matters

Professions are complete progression paths, not secondary activities.

A player should be able to build an identity around their chosen profession and feel equally valued within the world.

---

## Memories Over Power

The game rewards experiences, discoveries, friendships, collections, and accomplishments more than raw power.

The goal is not to become the strongest player.

The goal is to build a story worth remembering.
