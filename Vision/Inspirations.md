# Inspirations


## MMORPG Inspirations
  
### [[Nostale]]  
  
- Strong community identity  
- Family (guild) system  
- Casual social interactions  
- Relaxed progression  
  
### [[Dofus]]  
  
- Living world  
- Strong profession systems  
- Exploration-driven gameplay  
- Player interaction as a core pillar  
  
### [[Dragonica]]  
  
- Fun combat  
- Accessible group content  
- Memorable atmosphere  
  
### [[Elsword]]  
  
- Distinct class identities  
- Dynamic gameplay  
- Character progression variety  
  
### [[World of Warcraft]]  
  
- Sense of adventure  
- World exploration  
- Dungeon cooperation  
- Early community-driven gameplay  
  
---  
  
## Modern Inspirations  
  
### [[Hypixel Skyblock]]  
  
- Horizontal progression  
- Multiple progression paths  
- Long-term account development  
- Collection systems  
  
### [[Black Desert Online]]  
  
- Character-bound progression  
- Strong life skills  
- Equipment as personal progression rather than tradeable wealth  
  
---  
  
## Game Design Inspirations  
  
### [[FTL]]  
  
- Meaningful choices  
- Emergent stories  
- Replayability through randomness  
  
### [[The Binding of Isaac]]  
  
- Unique runs  
- Build diversity  
- Unexpected situations  
  
### [[Balatro]]  
  
- Fun-first design  
- Endless combinations  
- Replayability through decision making  
  
---  
  
## What We Want To Recreate
  
We are not trying to copy any specific game.  
  
We want to recreate:  
  
- The feeling of discovering a new world  
- The excitement of meeting new people  
- The satisfaction of progressing together  
- The joy of small adventures  
- The social atmosphere of early online games  
  
These emotions are the true inspiration behind the project.
