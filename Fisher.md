# Overview

The fisher's job is to find fish and treasure. Their job will be one of the most active ones. The player will need to search for fishing spots and fight with fishes that bite the bait.

# Gameplay

- Setting fishing nets and crab traps => Passive
- Randomly fish in river or ocean => Solo
- Boat expedition => Group / Solo

## Ideas

- Osu-mania-style mini game when a fish bites
- Boat expedition is a mix between Sea of thieves & Zelda wind waker
	- Fishing spots will need to be scanned for, and once one is found, there is limited time to exploit it
