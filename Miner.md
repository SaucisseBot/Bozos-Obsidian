# Overview

The miner's job is focused on balancing getting a big amount of ore and a small amount of gems and processing them.

The job is rather slow and steady. The gameplay mechanics should be based on resolving puzzles and not reactivity.

# Gameplay

* Ore and gem processing => Passive
* Outdoor ore rotation => solo
* Procedural mine shaft exploration => group / solo

## Ideas

- Gems processing looks like the shape-matching mini-game in Mario Party
- Gem extraction looks like the fossil mini-game from Pokemon Diamond and Pearl
